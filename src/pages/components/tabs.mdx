## Best practices

### Be mindful of horizontal tab length

When more than 5 tabs are needed or the tab strings are lengthy, consider a vertical tab layout. Tab
labels are internationalized and many languages contain more characters per word than English.

Horizontal tabs do not wrap or overflow so anything that exceeds the width of the page will become
truncated and may hide important actions.

import tabHorizDo from '../../images/components-tabs-horizontal-do.png';
import tabHorizDont from '../../images/components-tabs-horizontal-dont.png';

<BestPractice>
  <Do imageSource={tabHorizDo}>Use a circular shape to represent a person.</Do>
  <Dont imageSource={tabHorizDont}>Use a long tab label that causes formatting problems.</Dont>
</BestPractice>

## Accessibility

### Automatic activation

Garden doesn't endorse the use of [automatic activation in
tabs](https://www.w3.org/TR/wai-aria-practices/examples/tabs/tabs-1/tabs.html). There is no way of
guaranteeing that tab content will not require a network request or page refresh and it will
inevitably cause inconsistency in tab interactions across products.
