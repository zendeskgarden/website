## Best practices

### Be mindful of horizontal tab length

When more than 5 tabs are needed or the tab strings are lengthy, consider a vertical tab layout. Tab
labels are internationalized and the number of characters per word is greatly varied. Horizontal
tabs do not wrap or overflow so anything that exceeds the width of the page will become truncated
and may hide important actions.

<BestPractice>
  <Do imageSource={props.data.tabHorizDo.children[0].publicURL}>
    <ul>
      <li>Write concise labels, ideally one word</li>
      <li>Account for responsive resizing with vertical Tabs</li>
    </ul>
  </Do>
  <Dont imageSource={props.data.tabHorizDont.children[0].publicURL}>
    <p>Don't use lengthy tab labels, this can cause tab content to overflow and become hidden.</p>
  </Dont>
</BestPractice>
