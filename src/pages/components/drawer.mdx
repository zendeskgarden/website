## How to use it

### Default

When the Drawer opens, focus moves to it so users who rely on a keyboard and/or
screen reader can access the content within. Focus is kept in the Drawer until
it is dismissed.

Users may be able to see the screen’s content when the Drawer is open but they
will only be able to interact with the Drawer or the page content at any one
time.
