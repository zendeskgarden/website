## How to use it well

### Select the right shape for the job

Use circles to represent people and rounded squares for objects, brands, or products. This helps a
user distinguish between the two when scanning a page.

<BestPractice>
  <Do imageSource={props.data.avatarShapeCircle.childFile.childImageSharp.gatsbyImageData}>
    <p>Use a circular Avatar for people.</p>
  </Do>
  <Do imageSource={props.data.avatarShapeSquare.childFile.childImageSharp.gatsbyImageData}>
    <p>Use a rounded square Avatar for objects, brands, or Zendesk products.</p>
  </Do>
</BestPractice>
